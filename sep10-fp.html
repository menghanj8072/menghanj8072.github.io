Freedom Project

What is Freedom Project?

The “Freedom Project” for SEP10 is all about learning new content on our own and reflecting our learning in a blog. The purpose of the blog is to show how I learn new content on my own.

For my project, I am making a website about animation using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Github. My blog will walk through my process of independently studying animation and making the website.
